## Spark SSL - why?

Let's explore the spark.ssl.enabled option!

*Unlike the other SSL settings for the UI, the RPC SSL is not automatically enabled if spark.ssl.enabled is set. It must be explicitly enabled, to ensure a safe migration path for users upgrading Spark versions.* [Spark Official Documentation](https://spark.apache.org/docs/latest/security.html)
